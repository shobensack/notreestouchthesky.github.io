Short Biography

No Trees Touch the Sky is a Philadelphia-based ambient post-hardcore band formed in September 2023. Blending elements of post-rock, alternative, and indie, their sound is reminiscent of acts like Circa Survive, Dredg, and Saosin. Known for their emotive lyrics and dynamic compositions, they've released singles such as "Enrhythm," "Sunrise," and "See This Through." The band continues to evolve, performing live and engaging with fans across various platforms .

Extended Biography

No Trees Touch the Sky emerged from the vibrant Philadelphia music scene in late 2023, born from the remnants of a previous project involving four of its five members. The addition of bassist Justin completed the lineup, solidifying their new direction. Their name, inspired by a desire to encapsulate the essence of their genre and perhaps a serendipitous fortune cookie message, reflects their ambition and the atmospheric quality of their music.

Drawing inspiration from the late '90s to mid-2010s post-hardcore era, the band infuses their sound with ambient textures and indie sensibilities. Their songwriting process is collaborative, often starting with a riff or melody and evolving through collective input, ensuring each track embodies the essence of the entire group.

Their discography includes singles like "Enrhythm," produced by Fred Mascherino (formerly of Taking Back Sunday), "Sunrise," and "See This Through." These tracks showcase their ability to blend mellow ambiances with intense crescendos, delivering emotionally charged performances that resonate with listeners .

Active on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Bandcamp, No Trees Touch the Sky maintains a strong connection with their audience. They continue to write new material, perform live, and contribute to the evolving landscape of post-hardcore music.
