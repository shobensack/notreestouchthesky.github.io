Extended Biography

No Trees Touch the Sky emerged from the vibrant Philadelphia music scene in late 2023, born from the remnants of a previous project involving four of its five members. Their name, inspired by a desire to encapsulate the essence of their genre and perhaps a serendipitous fortune cookie message, reflects their ambition and the atmospheric quality of their music.

Drawing inspiration from the late '90s to mid-2010s post-hardcore era, the band infuses their sound with ambient textures and indie sensibilities. Their songwriting process is collaborative, often starting with a riff or melody and evolving through collective input, ensuring each track embodies the essence of the entire group.

Their discography includes singles like "Chrysalis", produced by Stephen Angello (Diver Down Studios), "3 Visits," and "Enrythm." These tracks showcase their ability to blend mellow ambiances with intense crescendos, delivering emotionally charged performances that resonate with listeners.

No Trees Touch the Sky maintains a strong connection with their audience. They continue to write new material, perform live, and contribute to the evolving landscape of post-hardcore music.
