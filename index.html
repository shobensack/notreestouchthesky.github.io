About

No Trees Touch the Sky (NTTS) is a band that stems from Philadelphia, PA. With roots branching out from acts like Circa Survive, Incubus, Saosin, Deftones, The Mars Volta, among others, NTTS captures a blend of ambience and raw energy reminiscent of the early 2000s post-rock while also invoking fresh emotions.
